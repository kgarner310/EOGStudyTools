🧪 Force Quiz

If applied force = 5 N and friction = 5 N, what will happen to the object?
Balanced forces (5N = 5N, net = 0N) means NO change in motion! Object stays still if it was still, or keeps moving at the same speed if it was already moving. Newton's 1st Law!
A softball is hit and rolls across a grass field, gradually slowing down. Which force is causing this change in motion?
Friction! When the applied force (hit) stops, friction from the grass acts opposite to the ball's motion. Friction is an unbalanced force here (no opposing applied force) → ball slows and stops.
Gravity is classified as what type of force?
Non-contact force! Gravity acts at a distance — Earth pulls you down without physically touching you. Magnetic force and electric force are also non-contact. Applied force and friction are contact forces (require touch).
